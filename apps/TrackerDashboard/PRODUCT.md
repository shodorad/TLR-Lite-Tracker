# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Executives and higher management. Primary context: standups — the dashboard is opened on a screen in a meeting, glanced at collectively, not studied at a desk. Information must land in seconds, not after scanning.

## Product Purpose

Internal engineering progress tracker for TrackLynk Lite. Pulls live Jira data to surface UX, Backend, and Integration completion status across flows and modules. Success looks like: any exec can read the room at a glance — what's done, what's blocked, what's left.

## Brand Personality

Fast, sharp, minimal. Confident tool energy — every element earns its place. No decoration for its own sake.

## Anti-references

Not Jira's own UI: bloated chrome, bureaucratic density, visual noise that buries the signal. Not flashy SaaS analytics with gradient heroes and big-number theater. Not Google Sheets. Not a marketing dashboard dressed up as a tool.

## Design Principles

1. **Signal over chrome.** The most important number is always the most visible thing on screen. UI structure serves data, not the other way around.
2. **Standup-speed legibility.** If someone can't read a stat from two meters away in two seconds, the layout has failed.
3. **Earn every pixel.** No decorative elements, no filler copy, no empty space that doesn't create rhythm or breathing room.
4. **Confident defaults.** One clear way to read each section. No ambiguity about what's good, what's at risk, what's done.
5. **Tool, not theater.** This is a working instrument for decision-makers, not a dashboard designed to impress during demos.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. Emphasis on readability at distance (standup projection context). Status colors must not rely on hue alone — use weight, labels, or shape as secondary signals. No reduced-motion requirements specified; keep animations purposeful and fast.
